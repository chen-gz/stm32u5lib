Global frequency response
Figure 386 shows the global frequency response for a 16 kHz audio signal with a digital
microphone working at 1.024 MHz. The filter configuration is the following:
•

CIC order 4 or 5, with a decimation ratio of 16

•

Figure 387. Global frequency response

Figure 388 shows the in-band ripple for a 16 kHz audio signal with a digital microphone
working at 1.024 MHz. The filter configuration is the following:
•

CIC order 4 or 5, with a decimation ratio of 16

•

RSFLT enabled, with a decimation ratio of 4

•

HPF enabled with a cut-off frequency of 20 Hz

The resulting in-band ripple is ± 0.41 dB for CIC5, and ± 0.45 dB for CIC4.
The - 3 dB cut-off frequency is 7061 Hz.
