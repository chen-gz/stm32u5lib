16.3.5

Triggers on MDF1 or ADF1
From EXTI, ADF1, timers (TIM1/3/4/6/7/8/16) and LP timer (LPTIM1), to MDF1
From EXTI and MDF1 to ADF1

Purpose
General-purpose timers (TIM3/4/16), basic timers (TIM6/7), advanced-control timers
(TIM1/8), low-power timer (LPTIM1), EXTI (EXTI11/15) and ADF1 can be used to generate
a triggering event on MDF1 module and start an A/C conversion. In addition, EXTI15 and
MDF1 can trigger ADF1.
