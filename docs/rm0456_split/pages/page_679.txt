System errors as break signals to timers
From system errors to timers (TIM1/8/15/16/17)

Purpose
CSS, CPU lockup, SRAM2/3 ECC double errors, FLASH ECC double-error detection and
PVD can generate system errors in the form of timer break toward timers (TIM1/8/15/16/17).
The purpose of the break function is to protect power switches driven by PWM signals
generated by the timers.
