Bit 25 CWDGPF: Clear watchdog pre-alarm flag
This bit clears WDGPF in GXFXTIM_ISR.
0: No effect
1: WDGPF cleared

This bit clears WDGAF in GXFXTIM_ISR.

1: WDGAF cleared
