The SincN digital filter embedded into the ADF can be configurable in Sinc4 or Sinc5,
according to CICMOD:
•

If CICMOD[2:0] = 4, Sinc4 is selected.

•

If CICMOD[2:0] = 5, Sinc5 is selected.
